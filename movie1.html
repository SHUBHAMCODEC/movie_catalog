RRR

"RRR" is an epic Telugu-language period action drama film directed by S. S. Rajamouli, depicting a fictional narrative centered around two Indian revolutionaries, Alluri Sitarama Raju and Komaram Bheem, who fought against the British Raj and the Nizam of Hyderabad, respectively, in the 1920s. Starring N. T. Rama Rao Jr., Ram Charan, Alia Bhatt, Olivia Morris, and Ajay Devgn, the film portrays their courageous journey, sacrifices, and struggles against colonial oppression. Despite facing numerous delays, "RRR" was released in March 2022 to widespread acclaim, captivating audiences with its compelling storyline and stunning visuals.

Interstellar

"Interstellar" is a 2014 epic science fiction drama film directed by Christopher Nolan, co-written with his brother Jonathan. Starring Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain, Bill Irwin, Ellen Burstyn, Michael Caine, and Matt Damon, the movie is set in a dystopian future where Earth faces extinction due to blight and famine. It follows a group of astronauts who embark on a journey through a wormhole near Saturn to find a new home for humanity. The film, praised for its scientific accuracy and groundbreaking visual effects, received positive reviews and grossed over $681 million worldwide. Christopher Nolan shot "Interstellar" using a combination of 35 mm film and IMAX 70 mm photography, with extensive practical effects and minimal CGI. Filming took place in locations such as Alberta, Klaustur, and Los Angeles, with Iceland doubling for extraterrestrial planets. The screenplay, initially developed by Jonathan Nolan in 2007, was inspired by theoretical physics and underwent years of refinement before Nolan's involvement. Matthew McConaughey and Anne Hathaway were cast in the lead roles after Christopher Nolan saw their performances in other projects. Production wrapped up in December 2013, with a budget of $165 million.

Passenger

"Passengers" is a 2016 American science-fiction romance film directed by Morten Tyldum and written by Jon Spaihts. Starring Jennifer Lawrence and Chris Pratt, the movie tells the story of two passengers who awaken 90 years too early from their induced hibernation on an interstellar spacecraft traveling to a distant colony. Originally written by Spaihts in 2007, the script underwent years of development with various actors attached before Sony Pictures Entertainment acquired the rights in late 2014. Lawrence, Pratt, and Tyldum quickly joined the project, and filming took place from September 2015 to February 2016 in Atlanta. The film premiered on December 14, 2016, and received mixed reviews from critics, who praised the performances and production values but criticized the plot and characters. Despite the mixed reception, "Passengers" grossed $304 million worldwide and received nominations for Best Original Score and Best Production Design at the 89th Academy Awards.
